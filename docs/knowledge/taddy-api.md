# Podcast API

An API for developers to get details on over 4 million podcasts, 200 million episodes, episode transcripts, webhook notifications for new and updated podcasts and more.

### 

[](#971eaf6639054f6ca52bc0e23486c089 "What can you do with the API?")What can you do with the API?

-   Get details on any [podcast](/developers/podcast-api/get-podcast-series) or [episode](/developers/podcast-api/get-podcast-episode).

-   Blazing fast [full-text search](/developers/podcast-api/search) to find podcasts and episodes.

-   Get the [transcript](/developers/podcast-api/episode-transcripts) for any episode.

-   Get a [webhook event](/developers/podcast-api/webhooks) for new or updated podcast data, or set up a [webhook filter](/developers/podcast-api/webhook-filters) to get notified whenever your brand gets mentioned on an podcast.

-   A list of the [most popular podcasts](/developers/podcast-api/most-popular-podcasts) on Taddy Podcast API, plus the [daily top charts](/developers/podcast-api/get-top-charts) for podcasts and episodes.

-   Return the [latest episodes](/developers/podcast-api/latest-episodes-from-multiple-podcasts) for a list of podcasts (helps you easily build a podcast feed).

-   Get details on [multiple podcasts](/developers/podcast-api/get-multiple-podcast-series) or [multiple episodes](/developers/podcast-api/get-multiple-podcast-episodes) (returns an array instead of one item).

-   [Add a podcast](/developers/podcast-api/add-a-podcastseries-to-taddy) to our directory.

-   **Download our Docs:** [Download our docs](/developers/podcast-api/helpful-docs-for-ai-coding-tools) to give your AI coding tool useful context about Taddy API.

-   **Enterprise features:** [Bulk download all 4+ million podcasts](/developers/podcast-api/bulk-download-podcastseries) from our directory.

👋

If you are new to using Taddy’s API, **Get Started** with our **[🤖 Intro to Taddy’s API](/developers/intro-to-taddy-graphql-api)****.**

[](#12d5f87f7a2b43eba44f79a9b2df2add "API Reference:")API Reference:

[PodcastSeries](/developers/podcast-api/podcastseries) - Information on a podcast

[PodcastEpisode](/developers/podcast-api/podcastepisode) - Information on an episode

[iTunesInfo](/developers/podcast-api/itunesinfo) - Additional information from iTunes on a podcast

[Country](/developers/podcast-api/country) - The country in which the podcast is made

[Language](/developers/podcast-api/language) - The language spoken on the podcast

[Genre](/developers/podcast-api/genre) - The genre of the podcast

[SortOrder](/developers/podcast-api/sort-order) - The option to have returned items sorted by newest or oldest.

[PodcastContentType](/developers/podcast-api/podcast-content-type) - Helps you distinguish between audio and video podcasts

[PodcastSeriesType](/developers/podcast-api/podcast-series-type) - Helps you distinguish between episodic and serial podcasts

[PodcastEpisodeType](/developers/podcast-api/podcast-episode-type) - Helps you distinguish between episodic and serial podcasts

[TaddyType](/developers/podcast-api/taddytype) - The types of media available on Taddy

[TopChartsSource](/developers/podcast-api/top-charts-source) - A list of platforms you can get Top Charts data from

[TopChartsType](/developers/podcast-api/top-charts-type) - A list of possible categories for podcasts or episodes to be ranked in

[SearchContentType](/developers/podcast-api/search-content-type) - The types of media available to search for on Taddy

[SearchMatchType](/developers/podcast-api/search-match-by) - Choose between searching for all terms, some terms or frequency

[SearchSortOrder](/developers/podcast-api/search-sort-by) - The option to have search results be prioritized by exact term matching or popularity

[SearchRankingDetails](/developers/podcast-api/search-query-ranking-details) - Ranking details for each search result

[SearchResponseDetails](/developers/podcast-api/search-query-response-details) - Additional details for search request

[FeedRefreshDetails](/developers/podcast-api/feed-refresh-details) - Details on how often Taddy checks a podcast feed for updates

[WebhookFilter](/developers/podcast-api/webhook-filter-type) - Programmatically add, update or remove webhook filters.

[Person](/developers/podcast-api/person) - People listed on the podcast including their roles (Host, Guest, etc).

[PopularityRank](/developers/podcast-api/popularity-rank) - Returns how popular the podcast is

[PodcastSeriesTranscriptionStatus](/developers/podcast-api/podcastseries-transcription-status) - Check if Taddy is automatically generating transcripts and chapters for this podcast

[PodcastEpisodeTranscriptionStatus](/developers/podcast-api/podcastepisode-transcription-status) - Check the status of automatically generated transcripts

[TranscriptItem](/developers/podcast-api/episode-transcript-item) - Transcript details including text, timecodes and speaker info.

[TranscriptLink](/developers/podcast-api/episode-transcript-link) - Link details where you can download the transcript for an episode.

[Chapter](/developers/podcast-api/episode-chapter-item) - Details on the chapters for an episode.

[ChapterLink](/developers/podcast-api/episode-chapter-link) - Link details where you can download chapters for an episode.
